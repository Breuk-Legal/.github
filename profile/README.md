# Welcome to Breuk Legal ⚖️ 💻

> **Corporate law, engineered for agility, clarity, and the speed of modern business.**

### 🧬 Our Origin & DNA
Breuk was born from a shared conviction: the legal industry in Colombia needed a refactor. We bring together two profiles that rarely compile in the same environment:

*   **The Legal Rigor:** Rooted in traditional financial law, demanding high standards, fiduciary trust, and absolute precision.
*   **The System Architecture:** Driven by technology, DevSecOps, risk mitigation, and a deep understanding of how complex systems fail (and how to secure them).

In 2024, we co-founded **Docfasty**—a SaaS platform for contract automation. Not because it was simply the plan, but because we saw a real problem and solved it with technology. That experience taught us the full lifecycle of a legal product: from the development cycle and UX design to the pure satisfaction of deploying solutions that work. 

That startup DNA stays with us at Breuk.

### 🚀 What We Build Here
This organization is our Open Source hub. We believe in building in public and sharing tools that sit at the intersection of law and technology:

*   🤖 **AI Skills & Agents:** Tooling and orchestration for legal logic and workflow automation.
*   🛠️ **LegalTech Infrastructure:** Open solutions and integrations for modern startups.
*   🔒 **Secure Operations:** Best practices for handling sensitive legal data in the cloud.
